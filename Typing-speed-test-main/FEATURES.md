## üöÄ How to Use

1. **Open the App** - Navigate to http://localhost:5174
2. **Select Mode** - Choose time or word-based test
3. **Pick Duration** - Select 15s, 30s, 60s, or 120s
4. **Choose Theme** - Click theme icon to browse themes
5. **Click Text** - Focus the typing area
6. **Start Typing** - Test begins automatically
7. **View Results** - See your performance stats
8. **Try Again** - Click "Next Test" or restart button
